About Michio Kaku

Michio Kaku is a renowned theoretical physicist, futurist, and educator, known for his work in string theory. He is a professor of theoretical physics at the City College of New York and a prolific author, having written numerous best-selling books on physics and the future of technology.

Dr. Kaku inspired me as a kid. I would get stuck to the TV watching him speak with passion and excitement of what the future may bring. He has a way of explaining advanced concepts in such fun ways. Growing up as a kid in the suburbs of NYC, I always wanted to attend one of his classes at CCNY.
