Welcome to Melvin's Library!

Why?

After I started college, I regained intellectual curiosity. I listened to many podcasts to for life advice, and a common theme was to always read and learn. I had fun nerding out in my classes, so I figured it will be fun to nerd out during my free time. I have read a great deal of books in the past 2 years, and I appreciate the amount of knowledge and the many perspectives that exist in the world. Come learn and grow with me!
